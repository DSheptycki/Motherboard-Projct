Motherboards

What is a Motherboard

A Motherboard is a circuit board acting as the heart of a computer, connecting all the components and having connectors for other circuits. The motherboard allows all the components to communicate and transfer data between one another, without it no other pieces are useful. The motherboard is the central piece of the computer, and it ties everything together.
